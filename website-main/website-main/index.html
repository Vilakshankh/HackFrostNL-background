Welcome to Hack Frost NL 1.0!

Hack Frost NL is the first university-wide tech hackathon organized by students from Memorial University of Newfoundland. Hack Frost NL invites students and developers of all backgrounds to experience an exciting weekend where they can learn new skills, work on unique projects, and connect with the industry.

Hack Frost NL will provide you with the workshops, mentorship, and software you need in order to build a successful project. We will connect you to leaders in technology both in the local and national community. Our theme for this year will be Data Science.

We promise to bring you an unforgettable and fun weekend that cannot be missed!

Frequently Asked Questions

Am I eligible to participate?

Anyone is eligible to participate as a hacker whether you are a high school student, a college or university student, or a recent graduate within the last 12 months. If you are a professional, there are opportunities for mentors and judges. Everyone is welcome!

How much does it cost to participate in Hack Frost NL?

Hack Frost NL will be FREE for all participants.

I don’t have a strong coding experience, can I still participate?

Yes you can! We will run workshops throughout the event that will teach you essential skills, and we will make a dedicated prize category for first-time hackers provided they present a working prototype :)

What are the prizes?

Our team is currently looking for cool prizes that will offer winners valuable experiences.

Do I need a team?

You can totally fly solo if you prefer! Otherwise, you can participate in a team of up to 4 people. If you don’t have a team arranged before the event, we will be hosting a team-forming session at the beginning of our program.

Will there be swag and merchandise?

Yes, our team is working hard to bring you cool swag that will get you right in the hacker mood. We will be updating the website soon.
